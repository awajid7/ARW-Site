Cookie Policy

Effective Date: September 3, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Arrow Consolidated ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on [arrowconsolidated.com] (the "Site"). This policy should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, mobile) when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit and improve usability, analytics, and advertising.

Types of cookies we use

  • - Strictly necessary cookies — Required for the website to function (e.g., session cookies, security-related cookies). These cannot be turned off without affecting basic site functionality.
  • - Preference/Functional cookies — Remember choices you make (language, region, display preferences) so your experience is more personal.
  • - Analytics cookies — Help us understand how visitors use the site (pages visited, time on page, errors). Example providers: Google Analytics.
  • - Marketing/Advertising cookies — Used to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance. These may be set by third-party advertising partners.
  • ‎

Third-party cookies

We may allow third-party services to place cookies on the Site (for example analytics, advertising networks, video players, and social widgets). These third parties have their own cookie policies and controls — we recommend checking their privacy pages for more information.

Cookies we commonly set

  • _ga, _gid — Google Analytics identifiers used to distinguish users (analytics).
  • sessionid — Server session identifier used for logged-in behavior or form sessions (necessary).
  • cookieconsent_status — Stores whether you accepted/declined the cookie banner on the Site (preference).
  • ‎

Note: The exact cookie names may change depending on the tools you enable on the Site.

How long we keep cookies

Cookie lifetimes vary: some expire at the end of your session (when you close your browser), while others persist for months or years. Where applicable, the specific lifetime is determined by the cookie issuer (our systems or third-party providers).

How to manage or disable cookies

You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner on the Site (if shown), or control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality. Common browser cookie controls:

  • • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data
  • ‎

Children

We do not knowingly use cookies to collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date above and, where appropriate, notify users via the Site.
